What We Do

Built for the Worst Days
of a Homeowner's Life

Large-loss events require a specialist — not a handyman, not a franchise crew, not a general remodeler. Our entire practice is built around house fires and catastrophic water damage in Central Virginia.

House Fire & Smoke Damage

Structure fires, partial losses, total losses. We assess damage that adjusters routinely undervalue — char depth, smoke migration, heat damage to structural components, and hidden losses behind drywall and in HVAC systems.

Catastrophic Water Damage

Burst pipes, appliance failures, sewage backups, and storm-driven flooding that affect multiple systems. We don't dry things out and move on — we document and remediate everything the water touched.

Insurance Claim Expertise

As a licensed public adjuster and Class A contractor, Jason Bhattacharya bridges the gap between your insurance carrier and your actual repair costs. We speak the adjuster's language — and we know when they're wrong.

Who We Work With

We Are Not the Right Fit
for Every Claim

Our expertise is narrow by design. We take on fewer clients so we can do exceptional work on the claims that matter most.

We Handle
  • Single-family home fires — partial and total loss
  • Large-scale water damage affecting multiple systems
  • Storm damage with structural involvement
  • Sewage and contaminated water intrusion
  • Mold remediation from water loss events
  • Claims where the insurer's settlement is disputed
  • Properties in Central Virginia (see service area)
Not Our Specialty
  • Condominiums and HOA-governed units
  • Small or minor water damage (single room)
  • Routine repairs not related to a covered loss
  • Commercial or industrial properties
  • Cosmetic or maintenance work
  • Properties outside our Central Virginia footprint

Not sure if your loss qualifies? Call us. We'll tell you honestly whether your claim is a fit for Virginia Fire & Water — even if that means referring you elsewhere.
